Hi, I’m Emerson.

About me

I'm a 25 year old front-end developer based in New York.

I love effective and fast user interfaces that bring real value to people. I appreciate logical, user-centered design approaches. Therefore I strive to use technologies and methodologies that deliver the best possible user experience, trying to concentrate on simple, focused and concise development.

When I'm not designing things, I enjoy exercising, playing video games, drinking good coffe, playing guitar and more.
